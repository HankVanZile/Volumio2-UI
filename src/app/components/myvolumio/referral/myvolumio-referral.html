FAQ

Simply share your unique link to a friend who would like to create a MyVolumio account.

A referral is when your friend successfully signs up to MyVolumio via your unique referral link.

Your unique link does not expire and you can use it at any time, unless Volumio decides to cancel the referral campaign.

You can see a real-time count of your referral and the leaderboard on the Volumio UI while logged in on your account.

Once your friend (s) creates a MyVolumio account and you achieve a milestone, you'll receive your referral in a manner of minutes (check).

Only users with a MyVolumio FREE account must insert payment payment to claim their rewards.

We separated the rewards according to the subscription type to be fair to all users, and get the rewards according to the value of the subscription.

The limit does not exist! The more you make, the closer you are to being in the Top 1!
